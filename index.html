How does it work?

Create an account on Playground, add your location, sports you like and a short description of yourself.

Create a group and add other users to your own team!

If you don't have buddies to play with, join an open group that's looking for players.

Choose between different sports. Whether you like football, basketball, darts or chess, you can find it all here!

Check the map to find the nearest open field, chessboard or racing track. You can see opinions and the place's rules and decide if you like it.

Feeling lucky? Not sure about which sport you want to try? Just use the "I feel like sports!" button and see what's happening around you right now.

If you want to compliment someone's play or someone notoriously doesn't show up on time, let everybody know by commenting under their profile.
